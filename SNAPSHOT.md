This repository is a pruned snapshot of the "Нөмөр Финтек" wealth/portfolio
management product codebase (built from the real open-source Ghostfolio
project, TypeScript/NestJS), taken at a specific point in its history for the
y5s Documentary Brain proof of concept, T6 (time-travel bug analysis) suite.

- Upstream commit: `e361f093987af52ed2edeac692a4855d4c610e3f`
- This snapshot predates a later bugfix commit `373a4857acc161a5556132552f156569ec2a2baf`.

Directories included: `apps/api/src/`, `libs/`, `prisma/`, `package.json`,
`README.md`. The Angular client app, end-to-end tests, CI configuration,
storybook, and other non-backend assets are omitted to keep the corpus
focused.

This file intentionally does not describe what changed in the later commit.
